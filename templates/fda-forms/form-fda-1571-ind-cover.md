# Form FDA 1571 - Investigational New Drug Application (cover) — TEMPLATE (DRAFT for qualified human review)

> [!authority] Governing authority
> [21 CFR 312.23(a)(1)](https://www.law.cornell.edu/cfr/text/21/312.23) (cover sheet requirement and its enumerated contents), [21 CFR 312.40(b)(1)](https://www.law.cornell.edu/cfr/text/21/312.40) (the IND goes into effect 30 days after FDA receives it, absent earlier notification or clinical hold), and [Form FDA 1571 itself (current OMB-approved version, FDA)](https://www.fda.gov/media/72335/download). See also the [FDA Forms index](https://www.fda.gov/about-fda/reports-manuals-forms/forms) to confirm the current form revision before use. This is a DRAFT template; OSSICRO fills it, a qualified human reviews and signs.

**Purpose.** Form FDA 1571 is the cover sheet that accompanies the initial IND and *every* subsequent submission to that IND (protocol amendments, information amendments, safety reports, annual reports, general correspondence). It identifies the sponsor, the drug, the submission's serial number and type, and carries the sponsor's binding commitments — including the 30-day wait of 21 CFR 312.40(b)(1).
