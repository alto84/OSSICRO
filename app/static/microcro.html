Micro-CRO · 21 CFR 312.52

Transfer of Regulatory Obligations

A physician running a study as sponsor-investigator can transfer some sponsor obligations, in writing, to a thin accountable entity — a micro-CRO. Some obligations can never be transferred. OSSICRO enforces that line in code: it will refuse to draft a transfer of a non-delegable obligation.

Transferable sponsor obligations

Toggle each obligation. Anything not marked Transfer stays with the sponsor by operation of 21 CFR 312.52(a) — the "deemed-not-transferred" default.

Non-delegable — cannot be transferred by any TORO

These bind a human or an independent body. Try to transfer one to the CRO and watch OSSICRO refuse.
