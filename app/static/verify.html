OSSICRO Standalone package verifier

Verify an exported Route-3926 draft package

An OSSICRO package export bundles every rendered document together with a SHA-256 manifest. This page recomputes SHA-256 over each document's rendered text (UTF-8) — entirely in your browser — and compares it to the manifest. Nothing is uploaded; no server is contacted; you do not have to trust OSSICRO or the machine that produced the export. That is the point.

A VERIFIED result proves only that the document text is byte-identical to what was hashed at assembly. It does not make a draft anything other than a draft: signatures, consent, IRB concurrence, and submission to FDA remain non-delegable human acts.
